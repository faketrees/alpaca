Chocolate brownies with a twist

Double Headers

Ingredients

  • 50 light caramels (can also use butterscotch chips)
  • 2/3 cup evaporated milk
  • 1- 17 ½ ounce box of German Chocolate cake mix
  • ¾ cup melted butter or margarine
  • 1 cup chopped nuts
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • flour
  • your favorite pan grease

Directions

  1. Pre-heat Oven to 350 degrees
  2. Seperate evaporated milk into 1/3 cups
  3. Combine caramels and 1/3 cup evaporated milk in top of double boiler. Set aside on low to medium heat and stir occasionally
  4. Combine dry cake mix, butter, remaining 1/3 cup evaporated milk and nuts in large mixing bowl
  5. Stir until dough holds together
  6. Press half the dough into a greased and floured 13x9-inch baking pan, reserve remaining dough for topping
  7. Check caramel (it should be tting soft and have a rich, thick, and creamy texture
  8. Bake at 350 degrees for 6-8 minutes
  9. Sprinkle chocolate pieces over baked crust
  10. Spread caramel mixture over chocolate pieces
  11. Crumble reserved dough over caramel mixture
  12. Return to oven and bake 15-18 minutes longer
  13. Remove from oven and chill for 30 minutes
  14. Enjoy
